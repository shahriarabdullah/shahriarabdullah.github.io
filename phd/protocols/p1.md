## Step-by-Step Procedure
### Patterning side channels

1. Clean wafer thoroughly with acetone, IPA and methanol respectively  
2. Rinse with water  
3. Dry wafer with Nitrogen gun. To dry the spot held directly with tweezer, hold the wafer with hand, dry the tweezer, hold the wafer on another spot with tweezer, dry the wafer again.
4. Dehydration bake at 200C for 5 min
5. Spin coat with NR7 1500P negative photoresist. Saved as NR7 1500P program in MNC spin coater. (300 rpm, 3s > 3000 rpm > 5000 rpm/s, 45s)
6. After spin coating hold the wafer carefully with hand (don't use tweezer at this point. It'll destroy the coating)
8. Soft bake at 150C for 60s
9. Now we need to expose it under specific dose of UV light.
10. Take the mask holder tray located beside the machine and very carefully place the mask with chrome side up
11. Press the safety lock upward to (bottom of the mask) add an extra layer of protection
